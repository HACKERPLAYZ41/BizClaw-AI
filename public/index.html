WhatsApp Device Connection

BizClaw AI uses multiple concurrent container sockets (Baileys) to run client bots concurrently. You don't need to keep any browser or tab open to run your assistant.

  • Open your dashboard and go to the WhatsApp Status tab.
  • Wait for the secure pairing QR code to generate.
  • Open WhatsApp on your mobile phone, open settings, and select Linked Devices.
  • Tap Link a Device and scan the QR code.
  • Once paired, your status changes to a green Connected tag. The bot will automatically auto-resume whenever the server starts!
🤖

AI Agent Settings & Prompting

Configure your chatbot's character, FAQ replies, and business details. Load custom templates to save setup time.

  • Click the Load Template dropdown and select from 50 pre-built business niche templates (such as Minecraft Hosting, Sweet Shop, Gym, etc.).
  • Customize the generated text to insert your own product names, plans, pricing, and timing details.
  • Token Protection Guidelines: Keep prompt rules instructing the AI to answer in 1 to 2 sentences maximum. This keeps WhatsApp chats natural and saves your package message quota limits!
📞

Twilio Voice Call Setup

Enable your bot to make outbound phone calls to your mobile phone when customers ask for support or place order reservations.

  • Log in to your Twilio Dashboard and copy your Account SID and Auth Token.
  • Buy or reserve an active Twilio outbound phone number.
  • Insert these details into the fields in the dashboard settings and toggle Enable Voice Call Alerts.
  • In the Owner Number field, put your own mobile number with country code (e.g. +91XXXXXXXXXX).
  • Twilio's Text-to-Speech (TTS) engine will automatically synthesize a natural voice call warning you of active client tickets!
📊

CRM Leads & Live Chat

BizClaw AI parses all customer chats to detect leads and automatically logs them inside the CRM table.

  • Customer inquiries automatically generate a lead with an AI-generated interest summary.
  • Click View Chat in the CRM table to inspect the read-only WhatsApp message log.
  • Set status to "Resolved" to resume automatic AI responses for that customer thread.
